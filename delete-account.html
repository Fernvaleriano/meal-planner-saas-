Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your Zique Fitness Nutrition account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, please send an email to:

Email: support@ziquefitness.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include:
  • The email address associated with your account
  • Your full name
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • Meal plans and nutrition data
  • Saved recipes and favorites
  • Progress tracking and check-in history
  • Any uploaded photos or documents
Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please make sure to export any data you wish to keep before requesting deletion.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been permanently deleted.

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes for up to 30 days after deletion. After this period, all data is permanently removed from our systems.
