# INSTAGRAM MARKETING STRATEGY — ZIQUECOACH

## WHAT SEPARATES US FROM TRAINERIZE, TRUECOACH, & OTHERS

### 1. AI That Actually Creates — Not Just Templates
Trainerize and TrueCoach give coaches a library of pre-made templates. **We generate entirely new, personalized meal plans and workouts using Claude AI.** That's not a filter on a database — that's a brain that understands your client's goals, equipment, dietary restrictions, and builds something custom in seconds.

**Instagram angle:** Side-by-side — "Template-based platform: same generic plan for everyone" vs "Ziquecoach: AI-built plan tailored to YOUR client in 10 seconds"

### 2. Multi-Photo Meal Analysis (Nobody Does This)
Other platforms let clients type in what they ate. We let clients **snap 2-3 photos from different angles** and our AI estimates portions, macros, and ingredients from the visual. No tedious food search. No guessing serving sizes.

**Instagram angle:** Show a plate of food from 3 angles → AI output with macros. Caption: "Your clients hate logging food. We made it a 5-second photo."

### 3. Voice Commander — "Hey Ziquecoach, did Fernando finish his workout?"
**No competitor has this.** Coaches can use voice commands to navigate their dashboard, check on clients, and pull data — hands-free. This is a showstopper demo for Instagram Reels.

**Instagram angle:** Screen recording of coach saying "Check Fernando's workouts" and the app responding. Caption: "Your coaching dashboard just got a voice."

### 4. White-Label Everything — Your Brand, Not Ours
TrueCoach shows TrueCoach branding to your clients. We let coaches upload their logo, set their brand colors, and their clients see **the coach's brand** — not ours. Even emails go out branded.

**Instagram angle:** Split screen — "What your client sees on [Competitor]" (competitor logo everywhere) vs "What your client sees on Ziquecoach" (coach's own branding). Caption: "Your clients should see YOUR brand."

### 5. 3-Panel Visual Workout Builder — Built for Speed
Our workout builder has a 3-panel layout: search exercises on the left, drag-and-drop build in the middle, preview details on the right. Full workout in 5 minutes. Competitors use clunky form-based builders.

**Instagram angle:** Timelapse of building a full workout program in the 3-panel builder. Caption: "Full client workout. 5 minutes. Done."

### 6. Same App Everywhere — No Feature Gaps
Most platforms have a web app with full features and a mobile app with half the features. We use one codebase — PWA + native wrapper — so **everything works the same on phone, tablet, and desktop.** No "you can only do that on the web version."

**Instagram angle:** Same feature shown on phone and laptop side by side. Caption: "100% feature parity. Phone. Laptop. Anywhere."

### 7. Free Tier With REAL Features (Not a Bait-and-Switch)
Our free tier gives coaches 2 clients with **full AI meal generation, workout builder, food photo analysis** — everything. Competitors lock AI behind $100+/mo plans or cripple the free tier.

**Instagram angle:** Feature checklist comparing Free tier across platforms. Caption: "Free means free. AI meal plans, workouts, photo logging — all included."

### 8. Clean, Simple UX — Not Overwhelming
Coaches constantly complain that Trainerize is cluttered and TrueCoach has too many menus. We built one feature per page. Clean. Focused. Coaches and clients actually use the app instead of getting lost in it.

**Instagram angle:** Screenshot of our clean dashboard vs competitor's cluttered one (blurred). Caption: "Coaches shouldn't need a tutorial to use their own tools."

### 9. Supplement Protocol Management
We let coaches create full supplement protocols with timing, dosage, images, and phases — and clients get a daily checklist. Most competitors don't touch supplements at all.

**Instagram angle:** Client view of supplement checklist with check marks. Caption: "Your clients actually take their supplements now."

### 10. No Dark Patterns — Honest Business
Clear cancellation. Transparent pricing. No buried upsells. We show this in our marketing because trust is a differentiator.
