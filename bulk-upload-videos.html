Filming rules for gyms — how clips should be shot
  • Film horizontal (landscape) — phone turned sideways, never portrait.
  • Keep each clip about 20 seconds — one clean rep or two, no talking.
  • Record at 1080p (Full HD). Do not use 4K — it's huge and gets shrunk anyway.
  • Steady shot, whole body/movement in frame, good lighting, plain background.
  • One exercise per clip. Name the file after the exercise so it's easy to sort.
