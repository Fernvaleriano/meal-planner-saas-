FAQ

Coaches in transition ask these first.

Isn’t AI going to replace coaches anyway?

No — not the good ones. AI replaces information delivery. It can’t hold someone accountable, read their tone in a check-in, or get them to the gym on a hard day. Zique gives you the AI plus the human edge.

How exactly does the gym check-in work?

Client taps Check-In. The camera opens (no photo library access). They take a selfie or a gym photo. Two buttons: retake or submit. No edits. No filter. The picture is time-stamped and surfaces in your coach feed instantly.

What does the AI co-coach actually do for me?

It pulls answers from your entire roster the way you’d ask another coach: "Who's plateaued?", "Who hit a PR?", "Show me missed check-ins this week." Instead of clicking through 17 profiles, you get the answer in seconds.

Do clients have to download a separate app?

No. Clients use a branded web app that lives on their phone. Camera, logging, messaging — everything works without a separate download. Native apps are on the way.

Will my brand stay front and center?

Yes. Logo, colors, and tone are yours. The platform feels like your premium app to clients — not like a generic SaaS tool with your name pinned on top.

What if I’m moving from another platform?

The Growth and Professional tiers include guided onboarding. We’ll help you migrate clients, plans, and templates so day one feels like a clean restart, not a setup project.
