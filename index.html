Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.

Our AI analyzes your client's goals, preferences, and restrictions to generate personalized meal plans. You can customize and adjust any plan before sharing.

Yes, we take security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Yes! You can easily add clients manually or contact our support team for bulk import assistance if you have many clients to migrate.
