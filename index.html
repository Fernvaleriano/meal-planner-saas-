Why would a client hire me when AI is free?

Because clients who try the free version mostly quit. Self-service apps have a brutal drop-off problem. People rehire coaches because they want someone in their corner, not another tab on their phone. Ziquecoach is what makes it obvious to your client that you’re the kind of coach worth paying for.

How does the gym check-in actually work?

Client taps Check-In. The camera opens, with no access to the photo library. They take a selfie or a quick gym shot. Two buttons: retake or submit. No edits, no filters. The picture lands in your coach feed time-stamped, the second they tap submit.

What does the AI co-coach actually do for me?

It answers questions about your roster the way another coach would. "Who plateaued?" "Who hit a PR?" "Anyone miss check-ins this week?" Ten seconds, no clicking through 17 profiles to find one number.

Do clients have to download a separate app?

No. Clients use a branded web app that saves to their home screen. Camera, logging, messaging, all of it works without a separate download. Native apps are on the way.

Will my brand stay front and center?

Yes. Your logo, your colors, your tone. To your clients it feels like your app, not a generic SaaS tool with your name slapped on the top.

What if I’m switching from another platform?

Growth and Professional include guided onboarding. We help you bring over clients, plans, and templates so day one feels like a fresh start, not a setup chore.
