5-Day Hypertrophy Program — Phase 2
Goal: Muscle Growth / Hypertrophy
Level: Intermediate
Days/Week: 5
Instructions: 5 days/week, 4 weeks, intermediate level. Phase 2 progression of the 5-Day Hypertrophy Program — heavier compound lifts (lower reps, more working sets), fresh exercise variations, and matched cooldown stretches.
